About me

My name is Aaron Booth. I am 30 years old and I have a wife and three kids I currently work a full-time job and am enrolled in this class. I also am a part-time crossfit coach It has been my dream to always be a programmer, but when I was 20 years old I had my first child and being a new parent with full time school and a full time job was overwhelming. I had no choice but to commit to providing for my family by working my way up in retail. Every year I try to stick by a new years resolution, previously it was going from 230lb to 160. I have always dreamed of going back to school, but was always too scared to make the commitment.I heard about coding bootcamp a few years ago from a friend of mine who mastered in robotics at nc state.

It was very preliminary and bootcamps were just starting to be established and recognized so I was very hesitant to commit to something like that without knowing if a bootcamp program could get me the career I've dreamed of. This year I told myself I finally wanted to get out of retail and pursue other things. Although retail was the only thing I have had professional experience with.

I joined a crossfit gym a few years ago and with that you get to know a vast variety of people. One of my friends that I met she works at bank of america in a tech division I told her I had previous interest in programming and that i pursued it before i had kids.

When I talked to her she told me about the UNCC coding bootcamp program and that they hire from that and its a really good program.

With her telling me that it reaffirmed what my friend had told me about bootcamps a few years ago it just clicked. I knew I had to do this for myself and my family.
